THE MEMBERS

JISOO, JENNIE, ROSÉ, AND LISA – FOUR NAMES THAT ECHO WORLDWIDE, EMBODYING A FUSION OF TALENT, CHARISMA, AND STYLE THAT DEFIES BOUNDARIES. SINCE THEIR EXPLOSIVE DEBUT IN 2016, BLACKPINK HAS REWRITTEN THE RULES OF K-POP, LEAVING AN INDELIBLE MARK ON THE GLOBAL MUSIC SCENE.

LISA MANOBAN

Lisa, the group's lead rapper and main dancer, is a dynamo on stage with her unmatched charisma and explosive dance moves. As the group's only non-Korean member from Thailand, Lisa's presence adds diversity to BLACKPINK's international appeal. Her rap skills and dance prowess have solidified her status as the dancing queen of the group.

JENNIE KIM

Jennie, the group's rapper and fashion icon, is a trendsetter in every sense. With her fierce stage presence and a keen eye for style, she has become a fashion trailblazer both within and outside the K-pop world. Jennie's charisma and versatility make her a standout member of BLACKPINK.

ROSEANNE PARK

Rosé, the group's main vocalist and guitarist, enchants with her soulful voice and artistic flair. Hailing from New Zealand, Rosé brings a unique international flavor to BLACKPINK. Her emotive performances and musical talent have garnered her a dedicated global fanbase.

KIM JISOO

Jisoo, with her ethereal beauty and graceful presence, is the epitome of elegance. As BLACKPINK's oldest member, she brings a sense of maturity and charm to the group. Known for her powerful vocals and captivating visuals, Jisoo is a multifaceted artist who effortlessly commands attention.
